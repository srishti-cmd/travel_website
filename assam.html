ASSAM:"THE TEA CITY"

Assam is known for its rich Historical legacy. It houses 600 years old monuments of the Ahom dynasty which is considered as the longest ruling dynasty in Indian history.You will see great diversity in the cultures and traditions of Assam as there are various ethnic tribes and groups. Each has their own socio-cultural life including customs, culture, food, beliefs, languages and festivals.Some popular festivals of Assam are Baishagu, Bhihu, Rongker Chomangkan, Me-Dum Me-Phi, Ali-Ayeligang and Ambubachi Festival etc.





PLACES TO VISIT IN ASSAM

  • Kaziranga National Park: Home To One-horned Rhinoceros
  • Manas National Park: Biosphere Reserve
  • Kamakhya Temple: Religious Expedition
  • Majuli Island: Largest Riverine Island
  • Darang: Mythological Importance
  • Digboi: Oil City of Assam
